INSTRUCTIONS

Escape the CHUMS

You are the CAT, shown as C and c. Eat every dot, avoid the four A CHUMS, and use the large o dots to make them vulnerable as m.

Lateral jolt: shift the whole phone sharply left, right, up, or down without deliberately rotating it.

Rotational jolt: snap one edge downward while the opposite edge rises. The downward edge is the direction: left edge down means left, top edge down means up, and so on. Enable Invert rotational direction if your phone reports the gyro signs oppositely.

Both accepts either gesture. Gentle movement, hand jitter, and slow rotation are ignored. Let the phone settle before the next command.

Buttons: 8/4/6/2 or arrows/WASD. Space uses limited hyperspace fuel. Escape or P pauses.

You receive a new CAT every 10,000 points and on each new level. Two-player games alternate after a CAT is deleted.
